Our Products

Premium quality construction materials for all your building needs

Aggregates

Available in 10mm, 20mm, and 40mm sizes for various construction applications.

Use: RCC work, concrete mixing, road construction

Stone Chips

High-grade stone chips with uniform grading for superior concrete strength.

Use: Concrete production, foundation work

Crushed Sand (M-Sand)

Manufactured sand and river sand for plastering and concrete work.

Use: Plastering, concrete mixing, masonry

River Sand

Natural river sand with excellent fineness modulus for quality construction.

Use: Fine concrete work, plastering

GSB Material

Granular Sub-Base material for road construction and foundation work.

Use: Road base, pavement construction

Construction Fill

Quality fill material for land development and construction projects.

Use: Site preparation, backfilling
